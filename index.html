The 18 universities where students get the most access to teachers

The quality of your student experience at university depends how much access you get to the teaching staff.

The more professors, the more attention you and your studies will get, and the fewer students you'll have in your classes and lectures.

The Times Higher Education's World University Ranking 2016-2017 includes a ranking of universities' ratios of the number of students per member of staff.

The study ranks universities around the world, taking into account teaching, research, and citations of every university.

Here are the best UK universities for low student numbers per teacher.
